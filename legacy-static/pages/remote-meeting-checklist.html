Remote Meeting Checklist

Before the meeting

  • Clarify the goal.
  • Share the agenda.
  • Prepare links and docs.
  • Assign a note owner.

During the meeting

  • Capture decisions live.
  • Flag blockers.
  • Confirm deadlines.
  • Repeat the next step out loud.

After the meeting

  • Send a short summary.
  • Log action items.
  • Share recording or transcript if needed.
  • Schedule follow-up.

This kind of checklist content is useful for ad inventory and can later connect to downloadable templates or a remote-meeting recap tool.
