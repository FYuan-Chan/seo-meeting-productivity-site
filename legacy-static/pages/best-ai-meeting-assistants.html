How to choose the right AI meeting assistant

  • Summary quality: Can it create concise, readable decisions and next steps?
  • Action item extraction: Does it detect owner, task, and deadline cleanly?
  • Workflow fit: Does it integrate with your calendar, CRM, PM tool, or docs?
  • Language and accuracy: Can it handle accents, domain jargon, and noisy calls?
  • Pricing path: Does the free tier prove value before you commit?
